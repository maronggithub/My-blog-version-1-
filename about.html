Facial expression & Taste.

Title

Effecrs of modulated facial expression feedback on taste perception.

What it is about

Visual information affects the taste perception. However, it has been unclear how visual information indirectly reflects the characteristics of the food or drink perception. In this study, we focused on the facial expressions for those two kinds of taste-related expressions. We experimented to confirm whether there are some relations between the taste perception and modulated expressions. As a result, we found that the modulated sour and bitter expressions increased the intensity of sour and bitter tastes, suggesting that the taste-related expressions can crossmodally modulate the taste perception.
